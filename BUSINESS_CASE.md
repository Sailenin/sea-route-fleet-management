### Current Inefficiencies

1. **Distance-Only Optimization**
   - Picks shortest route regardless of fuel efficiency
   - Ignores port congestion
   - Misses carbon/cost trade-offs

2. **Static Planning**
   - Uses historical data, not real-time optimization
   - Manual route decisions for complex scenarios
   - Slow response to changing conditions

3. **Single-Metric Focus**
   - Optimizes cost OR speed OR carbon, not all three
   - Can't handle multi-objective scenarios
   - Suboptimal overall efficiency

**Cost Impact:** 
- Estimated 20-30% waste in fuel efficiency per route
- Represents $32-48M annual waste (for 100-vessel fleet)
